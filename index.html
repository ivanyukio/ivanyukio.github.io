State of the Art

A mobile app with the objective to connect people from all around the world through street art, with the options to browse street art photos taken by users scrolling through a map and clicking on pins that represent a piece of art and its respective location, pins from places you've been to are different from pins you've never visited before, or using a photo belt to simply see all the pictures without having to navigate a map. Users can contribute taking their own pictures of street art and the photo will automatically be tagged on the proper location on the map.
